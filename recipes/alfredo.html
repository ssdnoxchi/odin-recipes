Chicken Alfredo

Something about chicken alfredo just makes me want it all the time. It's creamy, light texture and strong cheesy flavour,and my love for chicken. This is my recipe for this perfect food, and I'm sharing it here, so you may taste this masterpiece too.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Fresh garlic
  • Heavy cream
  • Unsalted butter
  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Shredded parmesan cheese

This recipe is all about preference. That's why there are no measurements.

Steps

  1. Boil water in pot and season with salt.
  2. Season chicken pieces with salt and pepper and cook through in a skillet while adding garlic during the last minute.
  3. Cook pasta and reserve some pasta water for sauce.
  4. Simmer and reduce cream slightly, about 5 minutes, stirring it frequently.
  5. Stir butter into simmered cream to melt.
  6. Off heat toss in parmesan, pasta and 1/4 cup reserved pasta water. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Toss in cooked chicken. Thin with more pasta water as needed (it thickens as it sits).
  8. Serve immediately with desired garnish.
