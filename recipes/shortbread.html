Scottisch Shortbread

This scottisch shortbread recipe is authentic, yet so easy to make! The results alway amaze me and anyone I serve them to, because you'd think "oh they're just normal butter cookies", but once you try them, your mind blows.This recipe is loved not just in the UK, but accross the globe. It's a must try!

Ingredients

  • 375g Plain Flour/All Purpose
  • 250g Unsalted Butter
  • 125g Sugar
  • A pinch of Salt

Steps

  1. Simply mix all the ingredients together by hand until it's well combined.
  2. Roll out the dough until its about 1cm thick, and cut into small rectangles. Poke holes into the rectangles (does not have to be through the whole way).
  3. Bake at 160 degrees celcius for about 15-20 minutes (don't let them become brown).
